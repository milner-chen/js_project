Adventures of a Cat Lector

×

Help your neighborhood cat, Lector, in completing his chores!

1. Collect the items on his to-do list.

2. Collect in order! (Lector is a strange cat)

3. Lector will lose a heart if you don't.

4. He only has 3 hearts so be careful.

5. Empty the list before time runs out!

6. Move using the arrow keys (left, right, up).
